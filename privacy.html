Privacy Policy

For the ProfitGuard Returns Calculator Shopify App

Last Updated: September 1, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to ProfitGuard Returns Calculator ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you install and use our Shopify application (the "App").

Data We Collect

To provide our services, we access the following information from your Shopify store upon your authorization:

  • Order Information: We access order details, including line items, prices, and product costs. We do NOT access or store any personally identifiable customer information such as names, emails, or addresses.
  • Shop Information: We store your shop's domain and basic details to manage your account, subscription status, and trial period.

How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to perform the core functions of the App, manage your account, and provide analytics based on your aggregated and anonymized return history.

Data Storage and Security

Your Shop Information and Saved Return Analyses are securely stored on a PostgreSQL database hosted by Railway. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Data Sharing

We do not and will not ever share, sell, or rent your data with any third parties.

Your Rights

You may uninstall the App at any time to revoke our access to your Shopify data. Upon uninstallation, we will delete all of your saved data from our systems within 30 days.
