Due to the nature of our digital service, we do not offer refunds for subscription fees. All charges are final. If you are experiencing technical issues, please contact our support, and we will do our best to resolve the problem.

Absolutely. We only access the necessary order and product data to perform calculations. We never access or store sensitive customer information like names, emails, or addresses.
